# Vim as DSL

##

* `ciw`: change inner word
* `das`: delete around sentence

# Spell Checking

##
* `]s`: goto next misspelled word
* `[s`: goto previous misspelled word
* `zg`: mark word as good
* `zw`: mark word as wrong
* `z=`: suggests correct words
